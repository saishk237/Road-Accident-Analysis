Road Accident Analysis

Road accidents are a significant concern worldwide, causing countless injuries and fatalities each year. This mini-project aims to leverage the power of Microsoft Power BI to analyze road accident data, helping stakeholders make informed decisions and take proactive measures to enhance road safety.

The project begins by collecting and cleaning road accident data from reliable sources, such as government agencies or traffic authorities. Subsequently, Power BI will be used to create a user-friendly and interactive dashboard, allowing users to explore various facets of road accidents, including:

  1. Geographic Analysis: Visualize accident hotspots and identify areas with the highest incident rates.
  2. Time Trends: Explore accident patterns over time, such as daily, monthly, or seasonal variations.
  3. Contributing Factors: Analyze the factors contributing to accidents, such as weather conditions, road type, and driver demographics.
  4. Severity Assessment: Assess accident severity based on factors like the number of casualties and vehicles involved.
  5. Intervention Effectiveness: Evaluate the impact of safety measures, such as traffic signals or speed limits, on accident reduction.

The Power BI dashboard will provide an intuitive interface for users to filter and drill down into the data, making it a valuable tool for government agencies, law enforcement, and safety advocates. The project aims to enhance road safety by identifying trends and areas that require immediate attention, ultimately reducing the frequency and severity of road accidents.
